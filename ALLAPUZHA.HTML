Allapuzha

Carved out of the erstwhile Kottayam and Quilon districts, Alappuzha district
was formed on 17 August 1957 and consisted initially of seven taluks, namely Cherthala,
Ambalappuzha, Kuttanad, Thiruvalla, Chengannur, Karthikappally and Mavelikkara.
