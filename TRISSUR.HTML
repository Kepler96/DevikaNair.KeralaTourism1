Trissur

Thrissur is a city in the south Indian state of Kerala. It's known for sacred sites and
colorful festivals. In the center is Vadakkumnathan Temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva and
adorned with murals. The ornate, Indo-Gothic Our Lady of Dolours Basilica is nearby. To the north,
Thiruvambady Temple is home to several elephants. Sakthan Thampuran Palace houses an archaeology
museum with bronze statues and ancient coins.
